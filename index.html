Research Interest

My research focuses on harnessing beneficial plant-microbe interactions to advance sustainable agriculture and forestry, with three key directions:

  • Microbial community diversity

  • Ecological functions of root-associated microbiome

  • Excavation of ericoid mycorrhizal fungi
